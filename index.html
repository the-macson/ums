LPU Global: Credit Transfer option for the LPU students Apr 21,2021

Division of International Affairs brings great opportunity for the LPU students who want to get their credits transferred to partner foreign Universities in Canada, USA, UK, Australia &Dubai etc. Under credit transfer the students will be doing the following programs:

  • 2 years at LPU and rest 2/2.5 years at partner foreign university in case of Bachelor’s degree
  • 1 year at LPU and rest 1/1.5 year at partner Foreign university in case of Master’s degree and eventually get degree from partner foreign university after successful completion of the program in the foreign University.

Benefits:

  • Students are allowed to work 20 hours per week while studying.
  • Students can easily cover their living expenses with earnings from part time job
  • Students get PSWR (Post Study Work Rights) after successful completion of the program at foreign University
  • Cost Effective- Students can save their 50% of money as they would be paying 2 years fees only rather than 4 years fees
  • Opportunity to be global citizen
  • Option of PAID industrial training at United Kingdom

Eligibility:

  • 7 CGPA and above with no backlogs and reappearances at the time of application
  • IELTS score of 6.5 band overall not less than 6 in each module (Possibility of Waiver in UK Universities)
  • Enough funds to sustain yourself at the foreign University.
  • 2nd year students (2019 Batch)

Interested students can register on the link given below for the info-session

https://forms.gle/92XW5vMbft2KqTmd9

The invitation to session is by registration only. A separate email will be sent to the students who register.

For further information please contact division of International Affairs at studyabroad@lpu.co.in
